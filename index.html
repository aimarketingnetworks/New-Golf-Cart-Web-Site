Golf Cart Service & Lithium Battery Upgrades

What golf cart service do you provide?

We provide full golf cart service including routine maintenance, brake and tire work, electrical diagnostics, mechanical repairs, street-legal LSV compliance upgrades, lithium battery upgrades, and lead-acid battery replacement.

Do you upgrade golf carts to lithium batteries?

Yes. We install complete lithium battery replacement systems starting at $2,700.00, including professional installation. Lithium upgrades offer longer range, faster charging, and reduced maintenance compared to lead-acid packs.

Do you replace lead-acid golf cart batteries?

Yes. We replace lead-acid battery packs on golf carts that use traditional flooded or AGM batteries. We test charging systems and ensure your cart is restored to reliable operating condition.

How much does a complete lithium battery upgrade cost?

Complete lithium battery replacement systems start at $2,700.00 with professional installation. Final pricing depends on your cart model, voltage requirements, and battery capacity. Contact us for a quote tailored to your golf cart.

What are the benefits of lithium vs. lead-acid golf cart batteries?

Lithium batteries offer longer range, faster charging, lighter weight, and virtually no watering or routine maintenance — a strong advantage in Nevada heat and for daily use in lake communities. Lead-acid batteries remain a lower upfront-cost option; we replace flooded and AGM packs when you prefer to stay with traditional batteries.

Do you perform street-legal LSV compliance upgrades?

Yes. We perform street-legal LSV compliance upgrades for neighborhood and low-speed vehicle use. Requirements vary by community and Nevada regulations — contact us to discuss what your cart needs for Henderson, Lake Las Vegas, or your HOA area.
