Golf Cart Dealer FAQ — Lake Las Vegas & Henderson, NV

Answers to common questions about our rentals, service, sales, location, and service area.

Where is Lake Las Vegas Golf Carts located?

We are located at 25 Via Bel Canto #105, Henderson NV 89011. Our primary service area is Lake Las Vegas, Nevada. We serve customers throughout Henderson and the greater Las Vegas area.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Lake Las Vegas, Henderson, Las Vegas, Summerlin, and the broader Clark County / greater Las Vegas metro area in Nevada.

Do you rent golf carts in Lake Las Vegas and Las Vegas?

Yes. Golf cart rentals are our primary service. We rent carts for Lake Las Vegas, Henderson, and throughout the Las Vegas area for weekends, events, weddings, tours, corporate use, and longer-term needs.

What golf cart service do you provide?

We provide full golf cart service including routine maintenance, brake and tire work, electrical diagnostics, mechanical repairs, street-legal LSV compliance upgrades, lithium battery upgrades, and lead-acid battery replacement.

Do you upgrade golf carts to lithium batteries?

Yes. We install complete lithium battery replacement systems starting at $2,700.00, including professional installation. Lithium upgrades offer longer range, faster charging, and reduced maintenance compared to lead-acid packs.

Do you replace lead-acid golf cart batteries?

Yes. We replace lead-acid battery packs on golf carts that use traditional flooded or AGM batteries. We test charging systems and ensure your cart is restored to reliable operating condition.

Do you sell new and used golf carts?

Yes. We sell new and used golf carts. New EPIC and ICON models are listed on our website. Used inventory changes regularly — call (702) 577-2222 for current availability.

Do you provide golf carts for businesses and corporate fleets?

Yes. We provide business and corporate fleet golf carts for resorts, commercial properties, HOAs, event venues, and organizations. We also rent multiple carts for corporate events and group bookings.

Do you sell personal and neighborhood golf carts?

Yes. We specialize in personal golf carts for individual owners and neighborhood golf carts suited for lake communities, master-planned developments, and HOA-regulated areas in the Las Vegas valley.
